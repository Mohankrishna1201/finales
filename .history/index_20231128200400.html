About The Event

About Us

E-Summit is a three day event, started as an initiative of the E-Cell of IIT Bhubaneswar. The Summit seeks to facilitate interaction among industrialists, funding agencies, seasoned and upcoming entrepreneurs, academia and students of the participating institutes. E-Summit’23 will include a gamut of activities such as entrepreneurial guest lectures, workshops and panel discussions with experts, startup internship fair and investors drive. E-summit, as a culture of IIT Bhubaneswar, has proved its worth in depleting the Chasm that exists between Entrepreneurship and management. It is the stage for future Industry leaders and Entrepreneurs to exercise their skills.

Theme

Digital Renaissance : entrepreneurial Resurgence

Sustainable entrepreneurship describes the nexus between sustainable development and entrepreneurship. Innovation is necessary for both sustainability and entrepreneurship, and when applied to these two aspects, it denotes a novel way of combining already-existing resources. It aspires to create viable market solutions and to act as agents who realise and exploit opportunities for sustainable development. The main focus of sustainable entrepreneurship is preserving nature, life support and community in the pursuit of options to bring future products, processes and services for gain into existence. The sustainable entrepreneurial process is typically triggered by entrepreneurs’ motivation of non-economic benefits and emotions: it is the compassion to support the well-being of others that creates a shared vision between the founders, the enterprise, and its members. We at E-Summit feel that “Sustainable development” is a demand that businesses must embrace sooner or later to be successful and even viable in the longer term. We believe that existing practices of business need to change for saving the planet, we know that we are the last generation that can end climate change, and with will and awareness in our youth, we are optimistic that we will reach that goal.

“Creating a strong business and building a better world are not conflicting goals – they are both essential ingredients for long-term success” -- William Clay Ford
